Eggs Benedict

What's about it?

Eggs Benedict is a power player in the world of brunches, but be prepared to work a bit for this gooey, decadent dish.

It's a far cry from simple cereal and milk, but it's nothing even a novice can't handle with some practice!

Ingredients (Hollandaise Sauce)

  • Egg Yolks
  • Lemon Juice
  • Cool Water
  • Worcestershire Sauce
  • White Pepper
  • Salt
  • Butter or Substitute

Ingredients (Eggs Benedict)

  • Distilled White Vinegar
  • Eggs
  • Canadian-style Bacon
  • English Muffin Breads
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Make the Hollandaise sauce by adding the ingredients in a pot above a double boiler. Remove from heat after the sauce is fully mixed.
  2. Preheat broiler 6 inches from heat source. You'll add the muffins here shortly.
  3. Poach Eggs in Pot. During this, lightly brown your canadian bacon on a skillet.
  4. Place muffins on baking sheet and into the oven.
  5. Place buttered muffins on plate, add bacon, a poached egg, then drizzle with hollandaise sauce. Enjoy!
