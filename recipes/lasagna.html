Lasagna

What's about it?

This lasagna recipe is one of the most popular on this site and contains a brilliant blend of italian sausage, ground beef and ricotta cheese. If you wish, this recipe is also compatible with vegetarian options such as lentils, mushrooms and peppers.

Follow these instructions, and you'll put the whole ready-meal aisle to shame! Just be prepared to mess up on the way.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and Garlic
  • Tomato Products
  • Spices and Seasonings (paprika, rosemary, black pepper)
  • An Egg
  • Cheeses (ricotta, mozzerella, parmesan)
  • Sugar
  • Lasagna Noodles

    • Steps

    1. Brown the meat

    2. Dice and mix the tomato products and onion, garlic and seasonings in with the sugar.

    3. Layer each ingredient in a pattern in your tray, starting with noodles, adding beef and sauce, then a layer of cheeses. Beat the egg and use it to coat the noodle and cheese layers, helping the cheese to stay put when you cut into it to eat.

    Place in the oven with foil for 25 minutes at 200 degrees celsius. Remove the foil afterwards and place back in for 15 minutes.

    Let it rest for 15 minutes and enjoy!
