Homemade Burger

What's about it?

This dish is a must know to fend off a whole family of judgy parents and hungry children. Consider this recipe an essential piece of your home invasion kit, alongside a baseball bat and pepper spray.

Make sure you've got your condiments at the ready too!

Ingredients

  • Beef Ground Chuck
  • Large Red Tomatoes
  • White Onion
  • Burger Lettuce
  • Any other condiments and toppings!

Steps

  1. Form your beef into small patties around 2 inches wide.
  2. Add patties to pan and cook on both sides, around 4 minutes each.
  3. Cut tomatoes and onions to taste.
  4. Remove cooked patties, adding the brioche buns to the pan briefly to toast them - the beef fat will enhance your bread!
  5. Place patty and toppings onto the bottom slice of your toasted bun. Sandwich toppings with the top bun. Enjoy!
